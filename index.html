Gecko Care

Crested Geckos, also known as eyelash geckos, are a type of New Caledonian gecko. They make for very fun and interesting pets.

They have very easy care requirements, making them ideal for beginning reptile hobbyists. They don't require live insects daily, like some other types of geckos. They mainly eat a powdered gecko diet made of fruit and black soldier fly larvae., while live insects make a wonderful, nutritious supplemental food!

They thrive in most any environment with proper humidity, but care and cleanup are even easier in a bioactive vivarium!
